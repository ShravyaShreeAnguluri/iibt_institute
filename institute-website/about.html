About IIBT

Leading the blockchain revolution through education, innovation, and industry collaboration

India's Premier Blockchain Institute

The Indian Institute of Blockchain Technology (IIBT), Hyderabad was established in 2020 with a vision to become a global leader in blockchain education and research. Located in the heart of India's technology capital, we are at the forefront of the blockchain revolution.

Our institute brings together academic excellence, industry expertise, and state-of-the-art infrastructure to provide world-class education in blockchain technology and its applications across various sectors including finance, healthcare, supply chain, and governance.

IIBT is committed to nurturing talent, fostering innovation, and building a strong blockchain ecosystem in India and beyond.
